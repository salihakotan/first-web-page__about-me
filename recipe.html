Vegan Brownie Recipe

Recipe: nefisyemektarifleri.com


Serving Size: Serves 4-6

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes



Ingredients for Vegan Brownie Recipe:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2/4 cup soy milk
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour

For the Chocolate:

  • 1 packet of Vegan dark chocolate
  • 4 tablespoons of vegetable solid fat

How to Make Vegan Brownies?

  1. First, mash the banana thoroughly with a fork until it becomes a smooth liquid.
  2. Then, add sugar and whisk until well combined.
  3. While they set aside, melt the solid fat in a saucepan and stir in the chocolate until melted.
  4. Once melted, add the milk and chocolate to the mixture, and finally add the flour and mix from bottom to top using a spatula.
  5. Grease a baking dish and lightly dust with flour, then pour in the mixture.
